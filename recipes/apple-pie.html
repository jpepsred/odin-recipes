Apple Pie

Description

This is a recipe I've been making since I was a young child. I used apples from my parents' orchard, but organic apples from your local famrers' market will be great too.

The recipe is simple, using only flower, butter, apples, sugar, and, optionally, a spice such as cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 4 Apples
  • Butter
  • flour
  • sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix flour and butter to form dough
  2. Roll pastry flat and line greased baking dish neatly with the pastry
  3. Slice apples and layer over pastry
  4. sprinkle sugar and cinnamon over top
