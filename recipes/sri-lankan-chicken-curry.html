Sri Lankan Chicken Curry

Description

I learned this recipe while travelling in South India. I stayed with a farmer from Sri Lanka, and he cooked a Curry for me with the most tender chicken I've ever tasted. I've tried my best to recreate the recipe over the years, and this is what I've come up with for an authentic Sri Lankan curry.

Thighs work best, but breasts will work too. The great thing about curries is how versatile they are--they can be served with other curries, bread, rice, lentils, you name it! I suggest serving this curry with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs (400g)
  • One onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Paprika, cumin, garam masala, cinnamon

Steps

  1. Dice chicken
  2. dice onion
  3. fry
  4. add spice
