Lasagna

Description

I learned this recipe from an Italian friend while in Highschool. He claims it's a recipe his nona invented by trial and error when trying to please the prince whose kitchen she worked in. He did have a habit of exagerating though!

The recipe uses common pamtry ingredients like tinned tomatoes and dried lasagna sheets, but can be improved greatly with a jar of tomatoes from your local Italian shop, and basil fresh from your windowsill.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • basil
  • onion
  • flour
  • milk
  • butter
  • Mince

Steps

  1. make tomato sauce
  2. make bechamel sauce
  3. mince with onions and garlic
  4. boil lasanga sheets
  5. layer ingredients
